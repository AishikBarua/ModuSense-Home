### 3Ô∏è‚É£ What is an Embedded System?
An **embedded system** is a small computer designed to perform **one specific task continuously**.

Examples:
- Smart lock
- Smart switch
- Microwave controller
- Smart home hub

#### Key Characteristics:
- Low power consumption
- Real-time operation
- Dedicated functionality

Smart home devices are **embedded systems by design**.

### 4Ô∏è‚É£ Microcontroller vs Microprocessor

#### Microprocessor (MPU)
- Contains only the CPU
- Requires external RAM, I/O devices
- Used in PCs and laptops

#### Microcontroller (MCU)
- CPU + RAM + Flash + GPIO on one chip
- Designed for embedded systems
- Low power and cost-effective

üìå **Interview-ready statement:**
> A microcontroller integrates CPU, memory, and I/O peripherals on a single chip, making it ideal for embedded and IoT applications.

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ What is ESP32?
ESP32 is a **powerful microcontroller** widely used in IoT and Smart Home products.

#### ESP32 Features:
- Dual-core CPU
- GPIO pins
- ADC (Analog to Digital Converter)
- Built-in Wi-Fi
- Built-in Bluetooth

#### Why ESP32 is Used in Smart Homes:
- Wireless connectivity
- Low power consumption
- Affordable and scalable
- Industry-accepted

ESP32 acts as the **central brain** of the smart home system.
